How to save ChatGPT & Claude chats to Notion

Want your AI conversations living in Notion next to the rest of your work? Ferry creates a clean Notion page from any ChatGPT or Claude chat — using a key you control, sent directly from your browser.

The 3-step version

  1. Create a Notion integration at notion.so/my-integrations, copy its token, and paste it into Ferry's settings along with the parent page ID. Share that page with the integration.
  2. Open a ChatGPT or Claude chat and click the Ferry icon.
  3. Choose "Notion" — Ferry builds a structured page (headings, code blocks, lists, quotes) under your parent page.
Your Notion token stays on your device. Ferry calls the Notion API from your browser, with your key — we never see it and never relay your content.

What the page looks like

Each turn becomes a heading (You / the assistant), with the body converted to native Notion blocks: real code blocks with language, bulleted lists, quotes and paragraphs — not a wall of pasted text. It reads like a page you'd write yourself.
