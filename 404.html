Questions

Does Ferry send my conversations anywhere?

No. Ferry runs in your browser and we never receive your conversations. For Pro, a minimal licensing service sees only an anonymous ID to check your access status with Stripe — never chat content. The only time a conversation leaves your device is when you send one to Obsidian, Notion or GitHub with your own credentials.

Which browsers work?

Chrome, Edge, Brave, Arc and Firefox — on Windows, macOS and Linux.

How does pricing work — is it a subscription?

Your choice. The 1, 3 and 6-month fares are prepaid — they don't auto-renew; access simply ends unless you buy again. The annual fare auto-renews at the lowest rate and you can cancel anytime. Either way, anything you've already exported is yours and stays on your device.

Can I get a refund?

Yes — 30 days, no questions asked. See Terms.
