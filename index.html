Questions

Does Ferry send my conversations anywhere?

No. Ferry runs in your browser and we never receive your conversations. For Pro, a minimal licensing service sees only an anonymous ID to check your access status with Stripe, never chat content. The only time a conversation leaves your device is when you send one to Obsidian, Notion or GitHub with your own credentials.

Which browsers work?

Chrome, Edge, Brave, Arc and Firefox. It works on Windows, macOS, and Linux.

How does pricing work, and is it a subscription?

Your choice. The 1, 3, and 6 month fares are prepaid, so they don't auto-renew. Access simply ends unless you buy again. The annual fare auto-renews at the lowest rate and you can cancel anytime. Either way, anything you've already exported is yours and stays on your device.

Can I get a refund?

Yes, 30 days, no questions asked. See Terms.
